You're invited!

Ancient items auction party

The details:

Location: Cork Opera House, Lavitt's Quay, Cork City

Date and time: September 19th 2018 at 7:45pm

  • This party gives someone a chance to donate an ancient item that's not in use. One can have the chance to sell the item to a trader and donate the proceeds to the MSF to continue saving lives
  • One gets to experience auctions and one gets to see the items put up in a museum. One learns how to bid and one learns how buying works.
  • Every guest that attends will help save someone's life by donating prized possessions.
  • The other events of the charity auction are a secret and the highest sale will earn an award.
