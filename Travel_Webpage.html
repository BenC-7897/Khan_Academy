Travel to Ireland

Ireland is a beautiful nation boasting spectacular landscapes, significant cultural heritage that sits beside a booming economy and magical rural locations

It's a safe country with mostly law-abiding and friendly citizens and one can reach the island nation by ferry and by plane.

  4. The National Botanic Gardens, Dublin

  6. The English Market, Cork
  7. Blarney Castle, Cork
  8. Glendalough, Wicklow
  9. Kilkenny Castle, Kilkenny
  10. Bridge of Peace, Drogheda
    To get around Ireland
  • One can rent a car
  • One can purchase a travel ticket and travel on the developed bus network in towns and cities
  • One can travel by trains between Dublin, Cork, Galway, Sligo, Drogheda, Waterford, Dundalk, Limerick and Kilkenny
  • One can fly between Dublin and Cork but it isn't recommended
  • Rent a bike to travel around the cities
