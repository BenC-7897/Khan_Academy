Middle class udon

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: Two people
Ingredients Quantity
Fresh udon noodles 400g
Sesame oil Two tablespoons
Thickly sliced onion One
Head white cabbage One quarter
Shiitake mushrooms Ten
Finely sliced Spring onions Four
Mirin Two tablespoons
Caster sugar One tablespoon
Worcestershire sauce One tablespoon
Lettuce Five pieces
Turkey slices Eight

Method

Step 1: Half-fill a cooking pot with cold water.

Step 2: Turn on the gas and place the pot on the stove

Step 3: Once the water is boiling, place the fresh udon noodles and add a bit of cold water to allow middle cooking

Step 4: Cook for two minutes and then pour the udon in the colander

Step 5: Heat a tablespoon of the oil and add the onions and cabbage. Sauté them for at least five minutes until they're soft

Step 6: Add the mushrooms and some spring onions and sauté them for another minute

Step 7: Pour the sesame oil into the mixture and the udon noodles from the colanderand stir them until they're sticky.

Step 8: Place some pieces of lettuce and some slices of turkey into the bowl to heat up

Middle class ramen

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves four people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken stock Seven millimetres
Garlic cloves Three
Soy sauce Four tablespoons
Worcestershire Sauce One tablespoons
Sliced ginger Thumb-sized
Chinese five spice Half a teaspoon
Chilli powder Pinch
White sugar One teaspoon
Ramen noodles 375g
Chicken breast 400g
Sesame oil Two teaspoons

Method

Step 1: Mix the chicken stock, three halved garlic cloves, the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, the ginger, the Chinese spice, the chilli powder and 300ml of water in a pot

Step 2: Bring this pot to the boil, reduce the heat and simmer this mixture for five minutes

Step 3: Taste a bit of the mixture. Add a teaspoon of caster sugar to make it sweeter

Step 4: Place the ramen noodles into the pot based on the pack's instructions and then place the pot's contents into a colander

Step 5: In a separate pan, fry the chicken breast until it turns brown

Step 6: Quarter the meat and the condiments so that it fits into four separate bowls

Step 7: Strain the chicken stock and then bring to the boil

Step 8: Divide the stocks into four
